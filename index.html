Meet Dr. Jenkins

A Dentist You Can Trust With Your Family

Dr. W. Bradford Jenkins, DMD, has dedicated his career to exceeding the expectations of patient care — one relationship at a time.

DMD — University of Mississippi Medical Center Former MDA President Delta State University ADA Board of Trustees
"I take the time to carefully get to know you as a person — listening and addressing your particular dental concerns. That relationship is the foundation of everything we do."

Born in Greenville and raised across the Delta, Dr. Jenkins graduated from Washington School in 1994, earned degrees in chemistry from Delta State University, and received his DMD in 2002. He has served as President of the Mississippi Dental Association and remains deeply rooted in the Indianola community through Rotary, First Baptist Church, and the Chamber of Commerce.

His thirst for knowledge and passion for dentistry — through continuing education, seminars, and professional leadership — makes him one of the top dentists in the Delta.
